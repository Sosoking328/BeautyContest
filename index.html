Awesome Features

Your Local Marketplace in your Language!

  • INTUITIVE DESIGN

    Freshly designed for one of a kind user experience.

  • One Click Posting

    As easy as 1-2-3, click post, fill title, description, location, upload pictures and post to the world. Simple as that.

  • Multiple Categories

    With over 50 categories and more to be added, you can browse all types of ads without missing anyting.

  • Easy to Use Filters

    Filter your search by keyword, distance and prices. It's that simple.

  • ROCKET SPEED!

    Without delay, you can search for ads around the world in rocket speed.

  • MULTILANGUAGE

    Sosokan is currently avaliable in English and Chinese and more being added soon.

  • FAVORITES AND SUBSCRIPTIONS

    Don't ever miss an ad. Mark your favorite for later use or subscribe to your favorite categories to get automatic updates.

  • IN APP MESSENGER

    Directly contact with fellow Sosokaner via in-App chat.
